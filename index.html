TALA LINK: Patient Information Management and Medical Personnel Scheduling System

A comprehensive web application designed to enhance efficiency in handling patient-related information and streamline scheduling processes for medical personnel in the Pediatrics Department.

Quezon City University
SBIT-4J Capstone Project
Dec 3, 2025

Project Overview

Our system aims to assist medical facilities by improving the management of patient records, personnel schedules, and operational workflows with AI-driven tools.

Patient Information Management

Centralized and secure system for efficient recording, updating, and retrieval of patient records in the Pediatrics Department.

Personnel Scheduling System

Interactive scheduling system that displays department-wide view of assigned shifts for doctors and nurses.

AI-Driven Clinical Decision Support

AI-powered recommendation module that analyzes patient data to provide safe referral recommendations.

Key Features

The system integrates multiple modules to address specific needs within the Pediatrics Department

Patient Queueing System

Efficiently manage patient flow by organizing waiting lists and bed assignment.

Risk Classification Module

Classifies patients' conditions into low, moderate, and high-risk levels for prioritization.

Chatbot for Assistance & Queries

AI-powered chatbot to assist with common queries and provide information.

Medical Certificates / Slip Generation

Automated generation of medical certificates and slips with proper formatting.

Role-Based Access Control

Secure access control for nurses, doctors, and administrators with appropriate permissions.

AI Specialist Recommendation

Analyzes patient laboratory results and symptoms to provide referral recommendations.

Objectives of the Study

The main and specific objectives guiding the development of TALA LINK system

Main Objective

To develop a Patient Information Management and Medical Personnel Scheduling System for the Pediatric Department of Tala Medical Hospital that organizes patient data, enhances scheduling, and improves patient care through technology.

Specific Objectives

1. Evaluate existing processes and identify challenges
2. Develop centralized patient information module
3. Implement AI-driven clinical decision support
4. Create efficient queuing module

Evaluation & Assessment

The system will be evaluated using TAM and ISO 25010 criteria including Functionality, Reliability, Usability, Efficiency, Maintainability, and Security standards.
